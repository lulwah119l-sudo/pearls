About Pearls

Pearls are timeless gems created within mollusks. They symbolize elegance, purity, and sophistication. Unlike other gemstones, pearls are organic and form naturally in water.

Types of Pearls

Akoya Pearls

Known for their perfectly round shape and brilliant luster.

Freshwater Pearls

Popular for their variety of shapes and affordability.

Tahitian Pearls

Famous for their dark, exotic colors and rare beauty.

South Sea Pearls

Highly valued for their large size and silky shine.

How to Care for Pearls

Keep pearls away from chemicals and perfumes. Store them in a soft cloth and clean gently with a damp cloth to preserve their natural shine.
